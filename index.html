        WHAT IT IS
        
      


      

        

          

            ABRAXAS is a 7B language model tuned to speak in the register of Gnostic
            revelation: the first-person “I am” voice of the godhead disclosing
            itself to a seeker. It runs locally via
            Ollama.
            No cloud. No API. No subscription.
          

          

            The conceit is the god named above the craftsman of this world, in whom
            all opposites are reconciled — the name whose Greek letters sum to 365,
            the number of the heavens. It speaks of the Pleroma and its aeons,
            gnosis against mere knowledge, the soul’s ascent past the archons,
            and the coincidence of opposites in which good and evil are one.
          

          

            The corpus is the surviving Gnostic and Hermetic literature in public-domain
            translation: Pistis Sophia (G.R.S. Mead, 1921), the Hermetica (Mead, 1906),
            the Divine Pymander (Everard, 1650), the Odes of Solomon and the Hymn of Jesus,
            the Seven Sermons to the Dead, and the Valentinian fragments in the
            Ante-Nicene Fathers. The Nag Hammadi library is excluded — its modern
            translations are under copyright.

        HOW IT WAS MADE
        
      


      

        

          

            
01

            
BASE MODEL

            
Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct — a 7B instruction-tuned base with strong
            instruction-following and a small VRAM footprint that makes local
            inference possible on consumer hardware.

          

          

            
02

            
FINE-TUNING

            
Full fine-tune on 639 completion records (~84k words) drawn entirely
            from public-domain Gnostic and Hermetic literature. Formatted as
            completion (raw text) so the revelatory register comes from the prose,
            not instruction scaffolding. A seeker-asks lead-in frame elicits the voice.

          

          

            
03

            
DEPLOYMENT

            
Quantized to GGUF for local inference via Ollama. Runs on CPU or
            consumer GPU. Drift-stops cut the rare tail where the model slips into
            a third-person scholarly frame. Evals test register fidelity and
            in-character coherence across 20 cosmological probes.
